Why limits exist

RayseOS uses Claude AI to generate your plans and content. Every generation has a real cost. Free tier limits exist so we can keep the product running sustainably and reinvest into making it better for paying founders. No venture capital. No games.

Questions
Can I cancel at any time? +
Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.
What happens to my plan if I downgrade? +
Your existing plan data is saved. You can view it but won't be able to generate new content or regenerate your plan on the free tier. Upgrade again and everything resumes immediately.
Is the $29 really worth it? +
One investor call booked from a post you almost didn't write pays for the entire year. The question isn't whether $29 is worth it. The question is what one inbound investor conversation is worth to you.
When does Founder tier investor matching launch? +
Investor matching and pitch deck generation are being built now and will launch to Founder tier subscribers first. Early subscribers get direct input into how it works.
Do you offer refunds? +
We don't offer refunds for partial billing periods. If you feel the product genuinely didn't work for you, email us at emmaire7@gmail.com and we'll review it case by case.
