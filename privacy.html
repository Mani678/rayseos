RayseOS is built by a solo founder. We collect only what we need to run the product, we do not sell your data, and we are straightforward about everything we do with your information.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or use RayseOS:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password when you sign up
  • Startup information — your startup name, description, industry, stage, funding goal, and target audience entered during onboarding
  • Usage data — how you interact with the platform, tasks you complete, metrics you log, and content you generate
  • Communications — if you contact us for support or feedback

We also collect limited technical information automatically: IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited. This helps us fix bugs and improve the product.

How we use your information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Generate your personalized 30-day fundraising plan
  • Power the AI content generation features using your startup context
  • Track your progress and Investor Signal Score
  • Send product updates and important account notifications
  • Improve the product and fix issues
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

Information sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — AI Model, Supabase (database and auth), and Vercel (hosting) process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect the safety of our users
  • Business transfer — if RayseOS is acquired, your information may transfer as part of that transaction

Your startup pitch, ideas, and business information are yours. We treat them as confidential and do not share them with investors or other founders.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored using Supabase, which provides encrypted storage and industry-standard security. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal reasons.

If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at emmaire7@gmail.com.

AI and content generation

RayseOS uses an AI Model to generate your fundraising plan and daily content. When you use these features, your startup context is sent to the API. This is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

We pass only the information necessary to generate your content — your startup name, description, industry, audience, and stage. We do not pass your email, password, or payment information to the AI layer.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use advertising cookies or sell cookie data to third parties.

Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at emmaire7@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
